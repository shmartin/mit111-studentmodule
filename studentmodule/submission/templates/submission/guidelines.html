Please read the following guidelines before submitting your research paper:

  • Submit your paper in PDF or DOC/DOCX format only.
  • The file size must not exceed 20MB.
  • Make sure the paper follows the required template format.
  • Include all necessary sections: Title, Abstract, Methodology, Results, Conclusion, References.
  • Check for grammar and plagiarism before submission.
  • Each submission will undergo a review process before acceptance.
  • You may be required to revise and resubmit based on panel feedback.
  • For technical issues, contact the Help Desk via email or phone.
