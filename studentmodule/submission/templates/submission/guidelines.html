Please read the following guidelines before submitting your research paper:

  • Submit your paper in PDF or DOC/DOCX format only, following the filename format: LastName_FirstName_Title.

  • Check for grammar and plagiarism before submission.

  • You may be required to revise and resubmit based on panel feedback.
